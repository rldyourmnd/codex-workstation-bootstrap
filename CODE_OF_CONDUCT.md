# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Keep discussions relevant and evidence-based.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or inflammatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, or issues that violate this code.

## Reporting

Report violations to `danilsilantyevwork@gmail.com`.
